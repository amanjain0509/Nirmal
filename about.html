Dev Bhoomi Frozen Food Products

Dev Bhoomi Frozen Food established in 2014, is engaged in the processing of fruits and vegetables through Individual Quick Freezing (IQF) technology.

We focus on providing great variety to customers and offer a delectable range of products to choose from : Green Peas, Corn, Cauliflower, Bean, Carrot, Litchi, Mango. In order to maintain an impeccable standard of quality,we strive to use the highest quality healthy ingredients nurtured and grown by people who cares about delivering quality and nature's goodness in every pack, every bite.

World class peas and corn processing plant in Kashipur (Uttarakhand) with a production capacity of 5,300 Metric Tonnes Per Annum (MTPA)

Its day-to-day operations are looked after by Mr Yogesh Jain and Mr Vinesh Jain, who have a combined experience of 50+ years in selling and purchasing of goods PAN India. The existing business network developed over years has helped escalate NIRMAL to greater heights in a short span of time.
